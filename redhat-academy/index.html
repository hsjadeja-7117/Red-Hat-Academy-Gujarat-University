What is Red Hat Academy?

Red Hat Academy is a no-cost program that brings enterprise open source technology into the classroom. Through a partnership between Red Hat and our university, students here get structured curriculum, hands-on labs, and industry-recognized certification exams in the same tools used by real IT teams around the world - Linux system administration, containers, cloud infrastructure, and automation.

Our chapter is run by a small student core team, supported by a faculty coordinator and guided by our Campus Ambassador, the student leader who first brought this program to our campus and formed the team you'll meet on the next page.

Whether you're aiming for a systems administration role, a DevOps career, or you just want to understand what actually runs the internet, Red Hat Academy gives you a structured, guided way to get there - with a community of students figuring it out alongside you.

Real Certifications

Work toward industry-recognized Red Hat certification exams, not just completion certificates.

Hands-On Labs

Practice in real Linux environments instead of only reading slides - you learn by doing.

Global Community

Join a worldwide network of Red Hat Academy students, alumni, and open source contributors.

Career Pathways

Build a skill set that maps directly to system administrator, cloud, and DevOps job roles.
